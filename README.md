Sales Performance Dashboard (Excel)
Project Overview
This interactive Excel dashboard provides a comprehensive analysis of 2,500 sales transactions recorded between March 15 and September 13, 2025. The project focuses on key performance indicators (KPIs) such as revenue, profit, and product-specific insights, demonstrating proficiency in data cleaning, KPI calculation, and data storytelling for informed business decision-making.
Dataset Details
Total Transactions: 2,500
Data Range: March 15, 2025 – September 13, 2025
Unique Products: 2,226
Key Metrics: Sales, Profit, Quantity, Profit Margin

Methodology and Process
Data Cleaning: Addressed data quality issues by removing blanks, standardizing product names, and ensuring consistent date parsing.
Data Transformation: Leveraged Excel's PivotTable functionality to aggregate data and calculate essential KPIs.
Dashboard Design: Developed an intuitive dashboard interface incorporating KPI tiles, dynamic charts for regional and category breakdowns, a monthly sales trend, and a prioritized list of top-performing products.
Insight Generation: Extracted actionable insights to guide strategic recommendations for inventory management, promotional campaigns, and regional marketing initiatives.
Dashboard Features
KPI Tiles: Visual representation of Total Sales, Profit, Units Sold, and Profit Margin.
Monthly Trend Chart: Illustrates sales performance from March to September 2025.
Regional and Category Breakdown Charts: Provides granular views of sales distribution.
Top 10 Product Subcategories: Identifies key product drivers.
Interactive Slicers: Enables dynamic filtering by Region, Category, and Month for personalized analysis.
Business Insights and Recommendations
Regional Focus: The East and West regions demonstrate the strongest sales performance, indicating an opportunity to prioritize marketing expenditure in these areas for maximized ROI.
Inventory and Supplier Management: "Stapler," "Paper," and "Tablet" are identified as top-performing subcategories. Recommendations include optimizing stock levels and negotiating more favorable supplier terms for these high-demand products.
Promotional Planning: June exhibits a significant sales peak. Strategic planning of promotions and marketing campaigns in advance of this seasonal demand could further boost sales performance.
Tools and Technologies
Microsoft Excel: Utilized advanced features including PivotTables, PivotCharts, Slicers, and Conditional Formatting for data analysis and visualization.
Data Analysis Techniques: Applied segmentation, KPI design, and trend analysis to derive meaningful business insights.
